Supply Chain Profile (Amitive Inc.)

Cloud-Based Supply Chain Profile is a framework for bringing data at scale from Demand-Supply Synchronization Engine, Forecasting Engine, Recommendation Engine into a single pane of glass for temporal analysis of the state of Supply Chain monitored across several locations simultaneously through live streaming data as well as historical data.

Orthera: Custom Orthotix Recommendations

Orthera is an Orthotics application deployed as kiosks at Points of Sale at major whole-sale stores to allow users to order orthotics based on spot-recommendations generated based on:

  • feet scans captured and analyzed for pressure sensistivity
  • medical conditions entered by the using a wizard
